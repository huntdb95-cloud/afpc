About All Florida Plumbing Contractors

A Florida-built plumbing company focused on craftsmanship, communication, and showing up when it matters.

Our Story

All Florida Plumbing Contractors was created to give homeowners and contractors a plumbing partner they can count on. We started with a small service truck and a big commitment: answer the phone, show up on time, and stand behind our work.

Over the years we’ve grown into a team of licensed plumbers, field supervisors, and project managers who understand both service calls and large-scale construction schedules. We know how important it is to keep projects moving, protect your property, and leave every job cleaner than we found it.

From emergency leak repairs to complete plumbing systems for new builds, AFPC is focused on doing the job right the first time.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Straightforward pricing and honest recommendations.
  • Quality: We use proven materials and methods that last.
  • Communication: We explain your options and keep you updated.
  • Respect: For your time, your property, and your budget.

Why Florida Chooses AFPC

  • Licensed & insured statewide plumbing contractors
  • Experience with homes, multi-family, and commercial projects
  • Responsive scheduling and emergency service options
  • Backed by 5-star reviews from customers and contractors

We take pride in representing Florida well on every job we step onto. When you see the AFPC logo, you’re getting a crew that knows the local codes, understands Florida’s climate, and treats your project like our own.

Meet the Owner

The face behind All Florida Plumbing Contractors.

Chris Carucci

Owner & Licensed Plumbing Contractor

Chris Carucci has built his career in the trades by doing things the right way, even when no one is looking. After years of working hands-on in Florida homes and job sites, he founded All Florida Plumbing Contractors to offer a company that customers and contractors could trust for the long haul.

Chris is involved in every part of the business—from reviewing estimates and walking projects to checking on workmanship in the field. When you call AFPC, you’re getting a team that reflects his values: honest communication, clean work, and respect for your time and property.

Whether it’s a simple repair or a complex commercial build, Chris makes sure the job is handled as if it were his own home or investment on the line.
